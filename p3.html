Project #3 Experimental Clock

Description

The passage of a few seconds could either be traceless or can be thrilling in pressing circumstances, especially for people who experiences time blindness. A minute could be so fast and so slow. This clock showvases this experience of relativeness and struggle with the accurate perception of the passage of time. The two big circles represents hour and minute, in close colors and trajectories it creates diffuculty to read and understand. With the ever-changing colors and flashes of the small circle whihci represents seconds, its presence and percievability also changes ramdomly.
